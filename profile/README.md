# ⚡ Suffolk Programming, AI & Research Club (SPARC)

Welcome to the official organization profile for **SPARC** at Suffolk University! We are a community of builders, developers, and researchers moving beyond classroom theory to build real things and stay plugged into where technology is actually heading.

Formerly known as Computational Science and Mathematics (CSMA), we rebranded in 2026 to focus heavily on modern software development, artificial intelligence, and engineering workflows. **All majors are welcome to join us!**

---

### 🚀 What We Do

* **AI Agentic Coding:** We explore and build software using cutting-edge AI-powered tools, agentic workflows, and modern development environments.
* **Shipping Real Products:** We collaborate on production-style software with real stakes, launching applications meant for actual users.
* **Tech Talks & Events:** We host dynamic group discussions, tech talks, and interactive vibe coding contests to highlight current trends in the tech landscape.
* **Career Pathways:** We connect members directly with industry opportunities, help prepare them for technical interviews, and cultivate pipeline opportunities for real-world internships.

---

### 🛠️ Featured Projects & Collaborations

Our catalog highlights software fully built and shipped by SPARC members:

* **SPARC Public Website:** Our central community hub built natively using Next.js, TypeScript, and responsive UI components.
* **CollegiateX Mobile App:** An exclusive corporate partnership in Fall 2025 where club members secured internships to design, build, and deploy mobile features and UX flows for an external startup portal.
* **Smart Campus Navigator & Club Finder:** An interactive custom campus mapping and interest-matching platform engineered to enhance the undergraduate student discovery experience.
